About Atari

Atari is more than just a name in entertainment. It's a promise of fun, innovation, and quality. We design products that bring families together and deliver authentic arcade excitement at home.

With a passion for creativity and commitment to excellence, Atari continues to lead the way in interactive entertainment. Our mission is simple: to make play accessible, exciting and unforgettable.

  • Expanding library of games and experiences
  • Dedicated to quality and customer satisfaction

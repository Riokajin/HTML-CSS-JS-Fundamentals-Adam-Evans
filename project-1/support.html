Frequently Asked Questions

How do I insert a cartridge?

Simply slide the cartridge into the slot until it clicks into place. Power on the console to begin play.

My joystick isn't responding. What should I do?

Check the connection to the console and ensure the cable is secure. Try a different port if available.

Is there a warranty?

Yes, the Atari Video Computer System comes with a one-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure the power supply is firmly connected.
  • Clean cartridge contacts with a soft cloth if games do not load.
  • Restart the console after changing cartridges.
  • Keep the system in a cool, dry place to avoid overheating.

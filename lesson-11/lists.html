Ordered List

  2. Add milk and mix further
  3. Add sugar, flour, and baking soda
  4. Mix all ingredients until smooth
  5. Heat pan with oil
  6. pour batter into 3 inch circles
  7. Flip after a minute or so
  8. remove from pan, add butter and maple syrup - enjoy
